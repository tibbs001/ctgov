EEG-related Clinical Trials


The information presented here has been made available by the Duke Clinical Research Institute using AACT, a publicly accessible, open-source, relational database that aggregates the content of ClinicalTrial.gov. For this presentation, ClinicalTrials.gov advanced query feature was used to identify trials associated with the keyword 'EEG' according to the NLM search algorithm. On May 22, 2016, the query returned 1,221 EEG-related trials conducted at 2,643 facilities. Unique trial IDs (NCT IDs) were used to retrieve detailed information about these EEG-related trials from the Aggregate Analysis of ClincalTrials.gov (AACT) system.


The AACT database, sponsored by the Clinical Trials Transformation Initiative (CTTI), is freely available in the cloud to anyone interested in studying ClinicalTrials.gov information and developing their own visualizations. To learn more about AACT, please take a look at the AACT 2.0 Wiki
